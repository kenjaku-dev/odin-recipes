Recipe is

  • Soak 4 tbsp fenugreek seeds and 1/2 cup green or brown lentils separately in cold water overnight. Drain before cooking. If short on time, cover with boiling water 1–2 hrs
  • Marinate 1 large chicken (quartered or whole) with: 3 large onions thinly sliced, 1/2 cup olive oil, 1 tbsp salt, 1 tbsp pepper, 1 1/2 tbsp ginger, 1 tbsp Ras el Hanout (or msakhen), 1 1/2 tsp turmeric, and 1 1/2 tsp crumbled saffron. Cover and refrigerate 6+ hours
  • put chicken + marinade in a heavy pot, cook covered 15–20 min until a thick sauce forms.
  • Add drained fenugreek, a handful each chopped parsley and cilantro, and 4 1/2 cups water.
  • Beldi (free-range) chicken: simmer 1 hr, then add lentils and cook another hour.
  • Regular chicken: add lentils now, simmer about 1 hr total until lentils are tender and chicken is soft
  • Add 1 1/2 tsp smen (preserved butter) at the end, swirl in. Taste for salt, add water if you want extra broth — Moroccans love it soupy
